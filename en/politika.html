Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to https://gptishka.shop and explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and what rights users have.

1. Data Controller

The data controller is the GPTishka website administration (the “Controller”). Contact for privacy requests: support@gptishka.shop.

2. What data we collect

  • Contact data you provide: email, Telegram, and information in support messages.
  • Order data: selected plan, amount, order/payment status, transaction identifiers.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser/device type, language, visited pages, date/time, referrer.
  • Cookie/localStorage data for website functionality and analytics.

We do not request or store account passwords or full bank card details.

3. Purposes of processing

  • Order processing and customer support.
  • Payment verification, refunds, fraud prevention and abuse protection.
  • Service quality improvements, analytics, and operational reliability.
  • Compliance with legal obligations.

4. Legal bases

  • User consent where required.
  • Contract performance (including public offer terms).
  • Legitimate interests of the Controller, balanced with user rights.
  • Legal compliance requirements.

5. Data sharing

We share data only when necessary and only to the extent required:

  • payment providers and banks (payments/refunds/anti-fraud);
  • hosting and infrastructure providers (service operation);
  • communication/support tools (email/messengers) upon your request;
  • public authorities where legally required.

We do not sell personal data.

6. Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, contract performance, and legal obligations. Data is deleted or anonymized when no longer needed.

7. Cookies and analytics

We use cookies and analytics technologies (including Yandex Metrika) to measure usage and improve the service. You can restrict cookies in your browser settings.

8. Data security

We apply legal, organizational, and technical safeguards, including:

  • encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS);
  • access control and role-based permissions;
  • event logging and incident monitoring;
  • backup and recovery procedures.

9. Your rights

  • request information about your personal data processing;
  • request correction, restriction, or deletion where legally applicable;
  • withdraw consent where processing is based on consent;
  • submit a complaint to a competent authority or court.

To exercise rights, email us at support@gptishka.shop.

10. Cross-border transfers

If cross-border transfer is required, we take reasonable measures to ensure compliance with applicable law and protection of data subjects’ rights.

11. Policy updates

We may update this Privacy Policy. The current version is always available at: https://gptishka.shop/en/politika.html.
